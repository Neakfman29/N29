English

Please do not violate the rules on Neak Discord server. If you violate them, the following warnings will occur:

  1. 1 warning: First warning
  2. 2 warnings: Timeout for 60 minutes
  3. 3 warnings: Timeout for 24 hours
  4. 4 warnings: Timeout for 1 week
  5. 5 warnings: Timeout for 28 days
  6. 6 warnings: Banned forever

If you (a member) see someone violating the rules, please report it through a ticket or forum.

Note: Warnings will reset to 0 one year after being issued.

Fatal Warn Points (FWPoints) — English

Fatal Warn Points (FWPoints)

English

A new warn point that CANNOT RESET like normal warn points. This occurs when you receive another warning after completing a punishment. Example: you get a 60-minute timeout, and after it ends, if you get another warning within a specific timeframe, you will receive FWPoints. Only the owner can assign FWPoints, and they do not come with a punishment.

Fatal warn point at this time is unused
